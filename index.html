TOMATO

Tomatoes offer a range of health benefits, including protecting heart, brain, and gut health. They are a good source of nutrients like vitamin C, potassium, and antioxidants, particularly lycopene. Lycopene is linked to improved heart health, cancer prevention, and protection against sunburns.........

POTATO

The potato's fiber, potassium, vitamin C, and vitamin B6 content, coupled with its lack of cholesterol, all support heart health. Potatoes contain significant amounts of fiber. Fiber helps lower the total amount of cholesterol in the blood, thereby decreasing the risk of heart disease......................
